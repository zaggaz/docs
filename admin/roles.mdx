Control what team members can see and do within your Unibound organization. There are two roles: **Admin** and **Member**.

## Role Types

- **Admin** — Full access to manage agents, channels, integrations, team members, and billing
- **Member** — View and manage leads, conversations, and view agents

## Permissions Matrix

| Action | Admin | Member |
|---|---|---|
| Manage agents | ✅ | ❌ |
| View leads | ✅ | ✅ |
| View conversations | ✅ | ✅ |
| Manage channels | ✅ | ❌ |
| Manage integrations | ✅ | ❌ |
| Invite members | ✅ | ❌ |
